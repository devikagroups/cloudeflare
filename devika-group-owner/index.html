The Foundation

Ankit Aggarwal's foundation in business and real estate was shaped by his family's multi-generational presence in the industry. He pursued his education with a focus on business management and urban development, preparing himself to carry forward and expand the legacy of Devika Group.

His early exposure to the intricacies of real estate development and construction ignited his passion for creating sustainable, landmark developments that blend modern innovation with timeless values.

Leadership Journey

Founding Vision

As the third-generation leader of Devika Group, Ankit Aggarwal inherited not just a business but a visionto revolutionize the real estate landscape of Delhi NCR through sustainable development and architectural excellence.

Building upon the strong foundation laid by his predecessors since 1954, he identified the need for modern, environmentally conscious construction practices at a time when sustainability was still an emerging concept in Indian real estate.

Expansion & Growth

Under Ankit's leadership, Devika Group has expanded its portfolio to include diverse projects spanning commercial offices, residential complexes, and mixed-use developments across Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, and Goa.

His strategic approach has focused on prime locationsNehru Place, Raj Nagar, Sector 110 Noida, and Sadar Bazaarcreating landmarks that have become synonymous with quality and reliability in the Delhi NCR real estate market.

Key milestones include the development of iconic projects such as Devika Skypers, Durga Tower, and Saraswati House, which have redefined the skyline of their respective locations.

Devika Group

70 Years of Legacy

Devika Group was founded in 1954, embarking on a journey that would span over seven decades and three generations of visionary leadership. What started as a modest construction venture has transformed into one of Delhi NCR's most respected real estate developers.

The group's remarkable journey includes the successful delivery of 35+ projects and the development of over 6 million square feet of prime real estate. With 11,000+ happy customers, Devika Group has earned an unparalleled reputation for trust, quality, and timely delivery.

Pioneering Green Building Concept

Devika Group gave birth to the concept of "Green Building" in Indiaa visionary approach that continues to shape every project. This commitment to environmental consciousness sets Devika Group apart in the industry, demonstrating that commercial success and ecological responsibility can go hand in hand.

Every project reflects this ethos, incorporating sustainable materials, energy-efficient systems, and eco-friendly design principles that minimize environmental impact while maximizing value for occupants.

Management Philosophy

Ankit Aggarwal's management philosophy is rooted in three core principles that have guided Devika Group's growth:

  • Legacy Through Quality - Every project is built with the conviction that it should outlast generations, using superior materials and construction practices that stand the test of time.
  • Sustainable Development - Environmental consciousness is not an afterthought but a foundational principle integrated into every aspect of planning and construction.
  • Customer Trust - Three generations of customers have placed their trust in Devika Group, and maintaining that trust through transparent dealings and reliable delivery remains paramount.

His leadership style emphasizes long-term thinking over short-term gains, ensuring that every decision contributes to the sustained growth and reputation of Devika Group.
