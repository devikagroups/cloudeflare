Devika Vibe 110

A Premium Lifestyle & Retail Destination in Noida

Overview

Devika Vibe 110 is a modern commercial hub designed to deliver a complete lifestyle experience in Noida. Spread across approximately 75,000 sq. ft., this development brings together luxury retail, curated dining, and immersive entertainment in one vibrant destination.

With over 1.3 lakh footfalls, 150+ shops, and 50+ brands, Vibe 110 is built to be more than just a shopping space. It is a destination where people come to explore, relax, and engage. Every element of the project is designed to create a dynamic environment that appeals to modern consumers.

Designed for High Footfall & Business Visibility

One of the key strengths of Devika Vibe 110 is its ability to attract and sustain high footfall. With an estimated 1.3+ lakh visitors, the project is positioned as a high-activity commercial destination in Noida. This consistent flow of people creates strong opportunities for retail brands and businesses to grow.

The layout of Vibe 110 is carefully planned to maximize visibility for every outlet. Open spaces, clear pathways, and strategic zoning ensure that visitors can easily explore different sections of the mall. This not only improves the overall experience but also increases the chances of customer engagement for businesses.

For brands and investors, this translates into better exposure, higher interaction, and stronger business potential. The combination of location advantage, curated tenant mix, and modern infrastructure makes Devika Vibe 110 a space where business performance and customer experience go hand in hand.

Lifestyle & Engagement

Dining: A Curated Culinary Journey

Dining at Vibe 110 goes beyond just food—it is about experience. The space offers a diverse range of culinary options, from classic favorites to international cuisines and innovative fusion dishes.

Each dining outlet is designed to create a unique atmosphere, allowing visitors to enjoy not just meals but memorable moments. Whether it’s casual dining or a relaxed outing with friends and family, the environment encourages people to spend more time and explore different flavors.

Entertainment That Engages

Entertainment is a key highlight of Devika Vibe 110. The project features an open-air entertainment zone, designed to host large-scale experiences and live events.

This space includes one of the biggest live screens in Noida, making it suitable for sports screenings, performances, and movie experiences. The combination of open-air design and modern infrastructure creates an engaging atmosphere where visitors can enjoy leisure and entertainment in a unique setting.

Kids Play Area: A Space for Joy and Learning

The kids play area at Vibe 110 is designed with safety and engagement in mind. It offers a vibrant and interactive environment where children can explore, play, and learn.

With colorful interiors, playful structures, and a variety of activities such as slides, swings, and soft play zones, the space caters to children of different age groups. It ensures that families visiting the mall have a complete and enjoyable experience.

Strategic Location Advantage

One of the strongest aspects of Devika Vibe 110 is its prime location in Sector 110, Noida. The project is surrounded by residential communities, educational institutions, and commercial hubs, ensuring a steady flow of visitors.

  • Mahamaya Flyover 5 to 7 minutes
  • Metro Station 5 to 7 minutes
  • Greater Noida Expressway 1 to 3 minutes
  • Yatharth Hospital 0 to 1 minute

This strategic positioning enhances accessibility and contributes to consistent footfall, making it a convenient destination for both daily needs and leisure activities.
