Architectural Vision & Urban Synergy

Devika Vibe 110 is engineered to transcend the traditional commercial mall concept, positioning itself as a high-street lifestyle ecosystem. Spanning a significant development area of 75,000 sq. ft., the project is an architectural response to the evolving consumer demand for "Experiential Retail." Every square foot is meticulously planned to optimize human circulation and maximize storefront visibility.

Unlike standard commercial blocks, Vibe 110 integrates wide, airy corridors with a structured vertical layout, ensuring that all 150+ retail units enjoy equitable exposure to high-velocity footfalls. It is an environment where global aesthetics meet local commercial energy, creating a resilient asset class for investors and business owners alike.

Curated Retail Mix

The Connectivity Benchmark

The commercial viability of Vibe 110 is underpinned by its position in the "Golden Quadrant" of Sector 110, Noida. Surrounded by high-density, affluent residential catchments, the project secures a permanent audience of retail-hungry consumers.

  • 1-3 Mins from Greater Noida Expressway
  • 0-1 Min from Yatharth Hospital
  • 5-7 Mins from Major Metro Interchanges
  • Strategic access to upcoming Jewar Airport

This strategic positioning enhances accessibility and contributes to consistent, multi-generational footfall, making it a convenient destination for both daily needs and luxury leisure activities.
